Amenities

We are in the business of renting pop up campers, so we ensure our campers make you a happy camper. Our little campers come with fine amenities like:

  • One queen sized bed
  • One full sized bed
  • Sink
  • Table with two benches

Rates

We offer two pricing options depending on how long you decide to rent.

Option 1 - Less than 3 Nights: $85/day

Option 2 - 3+ Nights: $75/day

Camper must be dropped off by 2:00pm on the final day unless cleared by one of the owners
